❓ FAQ
How everything works in Peralytics
📊 Core Features
The Overview is your financial dashboard. It shows 8 key metrics at a glance: monthly income, total expenses, net savings, savings rate, fixed costs, discretionary spending, daily budget, and wallet balance. It also has 4 charts — expense breakdown by category, income vs expenses vs savings, top spending categories, and a month-over-month trend line. Use the arrows at the top of the sidebar to switch between months.
Add all your income sources here — salary, freelance, side hustles, business income, etc. You can set each source as Monthly, Weekly, Bi-weekly, or Annual and Peralytics will automatically convert everything into a monthly figure for your calculations. Income is used to compute your savings rate, net savings, and daily budget across all other tabs.
Log recurring expenses like rent, groceries, subscriptions, and utilities here. Each expense is tagged as Fixed (same every month, like rent) or Variable (changes month to month, like food). You can also assign a category (Housing, Food, Transport, etc.) and an optional budget limit. When you add an expense, the timestamp is recorded automatically and the amount is instantly deducted from your Wallet so your cash balance stays accurate in real time.
The Wallet is your real-time cash tracker. Start by tapping Set to enter your current cash on hand. Every time you log an expense, the amount is automatically deducted. You can also manually log transactions — tap Money In for income/deposits and Money Out for purchases. Each transaction records the category, optional note, and exact date and time automatically. You can delete any transaction to reverse it from your balance.
Set savings goals like an Emergency Fund, new phone, vacation, or down payment. Enter a target amount, how much you've already saved, and an optional monthly contribution and deadline — Peralytics will tell you how many months until you hit your goal. Tap Add on any goal to deposit money into it. Every deposit earns XP, and hitting Saving Milestones (₱1K, ₱5K, ₱10K, etc.) triggers bonus XP and confetti! Completing a goal gives you 200 XP and unlocks the Goal Crusher badge.
Track any money you owe — personal loans, credit cards, money borrowed from friends. Enter the total owed, how much you've already paid, and a monthly payment amount. Peralytics calculates how many months until you're debt-free. Tap Pay to log payments — each payment earns 15 XP, and fully clearing a debt earns 150 XP plus the Debt Slayer badge and a confetti celebration!
Receivables track money that other people owe you. Add the person's name, the amount, an optional due date, and a reason. You can mark items as Pending, Partially Paid, or Fully Paid. Peralytics will also alert you when a receivable is overdue so you never forget to follow up. Use the coin icon to log partial collections without marking the full amount as received.
The Budget Board is a visual envelope-style budgeting system inspired by YNAB (You Need A Budget). Create budget envelopes for categories like Food, Transport, or Fun. Each envelope shows how much you've budgeted and how much you've spent. Tap Add spend on any envelope to log a purchase to it. The board shows you your total income, how much is assigned to envelopes, and how much is still unassigned.
Insights analyzes your financial data and gives you personalized tips and warnings. It checks your savings rate (target: 20%), your biggest spending category, whether your fixed costs are too high, whether you're over budget, your daily safe-to-spend amount, debt status, and even your current XP level and available gacha pulls. Visiting Insights also completes the "Review Insights" daily quest for +15 XP.
Tools contains five utilities:

• Calculator — A full general-purpose calculator.
• Hourly Rate Calculator — Enter your target monthly income to find out what hourly rate to charge clients.
• 50/30/20 Budget Planner — Enter your income to get a breakdown of how much to allocate to Needs, Wants, and Savings.
• Split Bill Calculator — Calculate how much each person owes, with optional tip and custom share percentages.
• Emergency Fund Checker — See how funded your 3-month and 6-month emergency funds are.
• Receipt Vault — Upload and tag photos of receipts and bills. Stored locally on your device, never uploaded anywhere.

Everything you do in Peralytics earns XP. As you accumulate XP you level up, from Level 1 Budol Beginner all the way to Level 10 Peralytics Legend. Leveling up awards a free Gacha pull! Your XP progress is shown in the sidebar bar at all times.

The Gacha lets you collect 17 unique Peralytics icons — from Common coins 🪙 to Legendary crowns 👑. You earn pulls by accumulating points (XP). In the Arcade tab, choose your difficulty: Easy (50 pts per pull), Medium (150 pts), or Hard (350 pts). Each rarity has a different drop rate — Common items drop most frequently, Legendary items are very rare. Duplicate items stack (shown as ×2, ×3, etc.). You also earn a free pull every time you level up!
There are 17 badges to earn, each rewarding +50 XP when unlocked. Badges are awarded automatically when you hit the right milestone — no need to claim them. Locked badges show ??? to keep things mysterious. Examples include: First Peso (add income), Goal Crusher (complete a goal), Debt Slayer (clear a debt), On Fire (3-day streak), Week Warrior (7-day streak), Collector (own 5 gacha items), and the ultimate Legend Status (reach Level 10).
Every day you open Peralytics, your streak grows. Using the app two days in a row starts a streak, and each consecutive day adds to it. Streak bonuses give you XP equal to 10 × your current streak count — so a 7-day streak gives +70 XP just for checking in! Missing a day resets your streak to 1. Streaks also unlock the On Fire, Week Warrior, and Month Master badges.
Every day there are 5 quests to complete for bonus XP: Daily Check-in (+10 XP, just open the app), Log a Transaction (+20 XP, record a wallet entry), Goal Progress (+30 XP, add funds to any goal), Review Insights (+15 XP, visit the Insights tab), and Debt Payment (+25 XP, log a payment). Completed quests show a ✅ and cannot be earned again until the next day.
💾 Data & Privacy
100% locally on your device. Peralytics uses your browser's localStorage — no accounts, no servers, no cloud. Your financial data never leaves your browser. This also means clearing your browser data will erase your Peralytics data, so it's a good idea to periodically export or back up the file. Receipts are stored as base64 images in localStorage, so uploading many large photos may fill up storage faster.
The month switcher in the top of the sidebar lets you view different months. Each month has its own income and expense records. When you switch months, the current month's data is saved, and you see the data for the selected month. Wallet balance, goals, debts, and receivables are shared across all months since they represent your current state — only income and expenses are month-specific.
